001 / facilities

dancehall

200 sqm with professional vinyl dance surface, changing room, 4 bathrooms and showers. fits 40-60 participants depending on activity.

the old house

a magical old Portuguese house. dorm (6 beds), 5 bedrooms, dining for 12, kitchen, mushroom lounge, small gym, library, meditation space.

new buildings

3 high-end bedrooms (2 en-suite), open kitchen and dining for 30 with a giant octopus couch. plus 2 bedrooms with a view.

the warehouse

1000 sqm for raves, art projects, whatever you dream up. 7 min walk on the other side of the land.

outdoor

natural swimming pond, wood-fired sauna, vegetable garden, chickens, trampoline on the hill, unlimited camping space.

infrastructure

fast wifi throughout, weekly professional cleaning, various co-working spaces, meditation room, gym with weights.

the blue house

a villa 5 min walk from the main venue that can be booked separately. 8 bedrooms, a dorm and sleeping capacity of up to 20 people.
